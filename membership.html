Common Questions

What do I get at the free tier?
Public chapters, story previews, and access to whatever goes up on YouTube.
How often is new content posted?

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, of course. What, do I look like your prison guard?
What's the difference between Devoted and The Bound (ignore the prison guard bit up above)?
Devoted gets you access to all the content. The Bound adds the lore, worldbuilding documents, direct Q&A, and your name in the credits of videos.
