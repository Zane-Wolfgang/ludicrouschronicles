It had been hours.

That’s what Zane reminded himself every tick of the clock as he scuffed his heels against stone. He’d been wandering the outskirts of the castle wall for who knows how truly long, praying it remained barren throughout the night so nobody saw the man stumbling through sheer throes of pain.

Hours.

He’d endured this for hours.

A constant pressure, crushing him into the ground, subconsciously repeating the order for him to submit. All he could do is groan, knees buckling as he was tossed into a cobbled wall, punching gasps drawing out of every breath he attempted.



“*Aauhhnnn*..” Another throe of his insides being gouged out. He felt sick—feverish, even, warm to the touch and sweating from every inch of skin. His vision blurred, duplicating various objects if he let himself go too far.

But the worst of it all, through his body being incinerated while he was wholly, completely alive, was that he was feeling pleasure stab from his stomach and infect him as though it were a drug—gods, he could feel it. Between his legs he was sure to garner a response soon enough, but he couldn’t afford the luxury of anything other than—



“AaAAUGH!”

Not until he *gives in*.

The ache was unbearable.

It was penciling back to his core, drawing circles around his failing guard. Taunting him. Pressing his boundaries. Whatever this feeling nestled so deep inside him was, it was intent on making him *shatter*. What ever it was, it needed its *way* and wouldn’t stop, even if he ended up wasting away to the dueling sensations tearing him apart and costing him his energy.



*It’s exactly…what it wants…*

He panted against the wall, unaware his lower half had long since met the floor. The stone was cool…he could press up against that and rest, right…?



*No..wait…no rest..* But the thought diminished just as fast, and his tense shoulders began to melt as pleasure was forcing him to succumb further. *Gods…. What’s going on…?*

*It feels… good…* He thinks, his eyes lidded…he swore the area wasn’t usually this dark…

But he couldn’t feel much else as at *last*, the last pulses of his consciousness withdraw…



And his body takes a fresh new breath of relief as *he* comes fourth. Zane…(?) takes a moment, letting the night air sink into his skin before rolling his shoulders and standing up…decidedly lumbering off into the night, threads lashing far behind his wake.
